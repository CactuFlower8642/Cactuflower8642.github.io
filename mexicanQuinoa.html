Mexican Cheesy Quinoa

Prep time: 15 mins
Cook/Wait time: 35 mins
Total time: 50 mins
Difficulty: 4/10
Ingredients




Directions:

Step 1: Cook the quinoa with 2 1/4 cup water in an instant pot on manual for 12 minutes.

Step 2: When the quinoa is cooked, move to a heat resistant glass tray.

Step 3: Add the beans, salsa, and cut mushrooms into the quinoa.

Step 4: Mix the ingridients.

Step 5: Sprinkle the desired amount of cheese on the top.

Step 6: Bake in the oven at 350 degrees for 15 minutes

Step 7: Take out of oven. Additional toppings like avacado can be added at this stage.

Step 8: Serve hot, Enjoy!
