Oreo Truffles

Prep time: 20 mins
Cook/Wait time: 1 hr 45 mins
Total time: 2 hr 5 mins mins
Difficulty: 3/10
Ingredients


Directions:

Step 1: Seperate the cookie part of the Oreos with the cream part

Step 2: Save the cream and 1 oreo for later and put the rest of the cookies in a plastic bag. Close the bag and crush them using a rolling pin.

Step 3: Once the cookies are crushed, add them, along with the creamcheese and oreo cream into a bowl.

Step 4: Mix the ingridients.

Step 5: Shape the mixture into truffle shaped balls(about the size of a ping pong ball). Store in the refrigerator for 45 minutes

Step 6: Crush the 1 remaining oreo in the same ziplock bag.

Step 7: Melt the bakers chocolate.

Step 8: Take balls out of refrigerator and coat in chocolate using two forks. Place on a plate with parchment paper and sprinkle the crushed oreo. Refrigerate for 1 hour.

Step 9: Take out of refrigerator, Enjoy!
